Recipe for Bicolanos Favorite "Laing"

Ingredients
  • 3.5 oz taro leaves dried
  • 6 cups coconut milk
  • 2 cups coconut cream
  • 1/2 cup shrimp paste bagoong or balaw
  • 8 oz pork belly thinly sliced
  • 5 to 7 pieces red chilies
  • 1 piece onion sliced
  • 1/2 cup sliced ginger
  • 8 cloves garlic crushed
Instructions
  1. Combine the coconut milk, pork, shrimp paste, ginger, onion, and garlic in a cooking pot. Heat the pot and let boil.
  2. Once the mixture starts to boil, gently stir to mix the ingredients. Cover the pot and simmer for 15 to 20 minutes.
  3. Stir once more and simmer for a while to prevent the ingredients from sticking to the bottom of the cooking pot.
  4. Add the dried taro leaves. Do not stir.
  5. Let the mixture in the leaves absorb the coconut milk. This takes about 20 to 30 minutes.
  6. You can gently press the leaves so that it can absorb more coconut milk.
  7. Once the leaves absorb the coconut milk, stir the leaves and then pour the coconut cream.
  8. Cook for 15 to 20 minutes.
  9. Pour the coconut cream into the cooking pot. Add the red chilies. Stir.
  10. Cook for 10 to 12 minutes.
  11. Transfer to a serving plate. Serve.
  12. Share and enjoy!
